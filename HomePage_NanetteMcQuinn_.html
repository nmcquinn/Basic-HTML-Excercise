Designer of Graphics. Writer of Code. Developer of Web

Nanette McQuinn is a Native of Richmond, Virginia. She attended college in Norfolk, Virginia where she earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design from Old Dominion University and a Master of Arts degree in Media Communication from Norfolk State University. After working in the Washington, D.C. area for over 10 years supporting IT and Engineering Federal Contracts she decided to pursue a new career in Web Development. Her current skill set includes: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Ajax and APIs, Node.js, Database Theory, C++, MySQL, Command Line and Git. When she isn't learning a new programming language or testing code, Nanette enjoys listening to 70's Funk and R&B, drinking dry red wine and streaming Nollywood movies.
